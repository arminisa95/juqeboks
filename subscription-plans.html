Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change or cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal through our secure payment processor Stripe.

Is there a free trial?

New users get a 14-day free trial of Premium. No credit card required to start your trial.

What happens to my music if I cancel?

Your music stays on JUKE forever! If you cancel, you'll keep your tracks but lose premium features.
